Our Cardiac Services

Prevention & Screening

Comprehensive Heart Check-ups

ECG, 2D Echo, TMT, Holter monitoring and blood tests to assess your heart risk profile and detect problems early.

Non-invasive

Advanced Cardiac Diagnostics

Echocardiography, stress tests, CT coronary angiography and cardiac MRI supported by experienced technicians and doctors.

Interventional

Angiography & Angioplasty

State-of-the-art cath-lab for coronary angiography, balloon angioplasty, stent placement and other minimally invasive cardiac procedures.

Long-term care

Heart Failure & Rehabilitation

Ongoing management, medication optimisation, lifestyle counselling and supervised exercise programmes for patients with chronic heart conditions.

Your Heart Care Journey

1
Initial Consultation Meet our cardiologist with your symptoms and past reports. A detailed history, examination and basic tests are performed.
2
Diagnostic Evaluation Based on your risk profile, you may undergo ECG, echo, TMT or angiography to understand the exact condition of your heart.
3
Treatment & Intervention You receive a personalised plan – medicines, lifestyle changes or procedures like angioplasty – with clear explanation of risks and benefits.
4
Follow-up & Lifestyle Support Regular follow-ups, cardiac rehab, diet guidance and exercise plans help you maintain a healthy heart and prevent recurrence.
