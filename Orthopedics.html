Our Orthopedic Services

Joint care

Sports medicine

Sports Injury Management

Treatment for ligament tears, meniscal injuries, shoulder dislocations and overuse injuries with arthroscopic procedures and rehab.

Spine

Spine & Back Care

Comprehensive care for slip disc, spinal stenosis, scoliosis and chronic back pain using conservative and surgical options.

Trauma

Fracture & Trauma Services

24/7 management of fractures, complex trauma, joint dislocations and polytrauma with image-guided fixation and early mobilisation.

Your Ortho Treatment Journey

1
Consultation & Examination Our orthopedic specialist evaluates your pain, movement and previous reports, and may advise X-rays or MRI scans.
2
Diagnosis & Treatment Plan You receive a clear diagnosis and options including medicines, physiotherapy, braces, injections or surgery, depending on your condition.
3
Procedure / Surgery (if needed) When surgery is required, our team prepares you with pre-op tests, anaesthesia evaluation and detailed counselling.
4
Rehabilitation & Follow-up Post-treatment physiotherapy, exercises and lifestyle guidance help you regain strength, flexibility and confidence in your movement.
