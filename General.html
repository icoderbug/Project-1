Your Surgery Journey at CareNest

1
Consultation & Diagnosis Meet our surgeon with your reports. We review your history, perform examinations and may order additional tests or imaging.
2
Pre-operative Assessment A detailed anaesthesia check-up, blood tests and fitness evaluation are done. You receive instructions on fasting, medicines and admission formalities.
3
Surgery Day Our team guides you to the operation theatre, monitors you throughout the procedure and shifts you safely to recovery or ICU based on your needs.
4
Post-operative Care & Follow-up Pain management, wound care, physiotherapy and diet counselling are provided. Follow-up visits ensure smooth healing and a return to normal activities.
