In an Emergency, Follow These Steps

1
Stay calm & ensure safety Move the person away from immediate danger (traffic, fire, electric source). Check if the surroundings are safe for both of you.
2
Call the emergency hotline Dial our 24/7 emergency number and share the patient’s condition, exact location, and contact details. Do not hang up until told to.
3
Provide basic first aid If trained, begin CPR or first aid. For bleeding, apply firm pressure with a clean cloth. Do not give food or water to an unconscious person.
4
Bring medical information Carry the patient’s ID, medical reports, prescriptions, and list of current medicines or allergies, if available, to speed up care.
