Our Neurology Services

Brain

Stroke & Brain Attack Treatment

Rapid stroke assessment with CT/MRI, clot-busting therapy, neuro-critical care and rehabilitation support.

Epilepsy

Seizure & Epilepsy Clinic

Evaluation with EEG, long-term monitoring and personalized treatment plans to control and prevent seizures.

Spine

Spine & Nerve Disorders

Treatment for slipped disc, neuropathy, sciatica, nerve compression and chronic spine pain.

Movement

Parkinson’s & Movement Disorders

Specialised care for Parkinson’s disease, tremors, dystonia and gait problems with long-term management.

Your Neurology Care Journey

1
Consultation & Neurological Exam A detailed evaluation of symptoms, reflexes, muscle strength, coordination and medical history.
2
Diagnostics & Imaging CT, MRI, EEG, EMG and lab tests are used to pinpoint the exact nature of the neurological issue.
3
Personalized Treatment Plan Medication, physiotherapy, lifestyle guidance or intervention depending on the condition and severity.
4
Rehab & Follow-ups Long-term monitoring, neuro-rehabilitation and routine check-ups ensure steady recovery and symptom control.
