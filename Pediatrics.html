Your Child’s Visit Flow

1
Registration & Triage Our staff records your child’s basic details, checks weight, temperature and vital signs, and notes the primary complaint.
2
Consultation with Pediatrician The doctor examines your child, reviews history and advises tests, medicines or vaccines as needed. Parents can ask all their questions.
3
Tests, Treatment or Vaccination Any blood tests, imaging or immunizations are done in a child-friendly way, with trained nurses to minimise discomfort.
4
Follow-up & Growth Tracking You receive a clear follow-up schedule, growth charts and reminders for future visits or upcoming vaccines.
